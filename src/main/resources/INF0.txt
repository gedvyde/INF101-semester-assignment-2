"Fadder" week:

You won't get anything done without sleep! Sleep is the currency
in the game. Purchase items using sleep to help you survive
life as a new student. To play click on the piece you want
to purchase, then click on the desired spot on the game board.

Items available:
- Bed: Take naps to generate sleep. (Price: 50)
- Stipend Gun: A student's best friend, providing financial
relief in times of need (Price: 100 Toughness: 300, Damage: 25)

"Fadder week" will give you a taste test of what life as 
a student can be. You will learn that both sleep and 
stipend are crucial for survival.

Press ENTER to continue.
